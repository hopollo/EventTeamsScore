# EventTeamsScore

Key | Action
------------ | -------------
**NumpadSuppr** & **$** & **S** | **Start** the match
**Numpad4** & **Left arrow** | **+1pts** left team
**Numpad6** & **Right arrow** | **+1pts** right team
**Numpad1** & **Numpad7** | **-1pts** left team
**Numpad3** & **Numpad9** | **-1pts** right team
**NumpadEnter** & **F** & **!** | **End** the match
